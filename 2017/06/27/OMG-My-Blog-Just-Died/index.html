Long Live My New Blog

Alas, my hosting service upgraded my server and my long-neglected Wordpress blog is doesn’t work anymore. I’m taking it as a sign that I need to pay more attention to such things so took the opportunity to give Hexo a spin.

So far? Good! I like the CLI tools, and I love typing in markdown. Plus the setup is fast: half hour ago I never heard of Hexo and now here I am blogging away. Down side, the themes are meh. Fine, I’m a front-end developer, I’ll make my own. Someday.

Now to type up the post that made me check my site in the first place: Webdata in React!

Update 10 Minutes Later

Wow. The deploy. Niiiiiice. So smooth. So git. I’m swooning a little.
